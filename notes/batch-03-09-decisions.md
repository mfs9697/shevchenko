# Editorial decisions for reports 3–9

Source pages: 15–22 of the MPTM 2026 Book of Abstracts, Part 2.

## General policy

The seven reports were converted and language-edited while preserving their stated methods, results, and conclusions. Titles, author spacing, institutional names, punctuation, references, and DOI presentation were normalized. No new scientific claims were introduced.

## Specific decisions

1. **Kaplan-turbine and spherical-tank reports.** Literal translation patterns and sentence fragments were rewritten as continuous technical English. The cited methods and reported engineering conclusions were retained.
2. **Residual-stress report.** The Ukrainian text was reorganized to remove repetition while preserving the three-stage computational procedure, the steel grade, and the conclusion concerning martensite-rich surface regions and compressive residual stress.
3. **Orthotropic-shell report.** The compressed source notation was normalized as the matrix equation `\mathbf{S}\boldsymbol{f}=\boldsymbol{q}`. The raster deflection plot was redrawn as a scalable vector figure with the same axes, ordering of curves, and qualitative values.
4. **Functionally graded shape-memory material.** In the rule of mixtures, the descriptions of `V_m` and `V_c` were aligned with the conventional subscripts: metal and ceramic volume fractions, respectively. The source text appears to reverse these descriptions.
5. **Connected triangular plates.** The three raster panels were redrawn as vector schematics. They preserve the two triangular plates, their dihedral angle, the folded finite-element model, and the limiting square configuration at `\varphi=180^\circ`.
6. **Equation anchors.** Because equation numbering restarts in each report, the shared style now provides report-specific hyperlink anchors while retaining visible equation numbers `1,2,\ldots` within each contribution.
7. **Email typography.** Email addresses are printed with `\nolinkurl` inside a `mailto` link so addresses containing underscores compile correctly.
