# Editorial decisions for reports 24–30

Source pages: 39–48 of the MPTM 2026 Book of Abstracts, Part 2.

1. **Finite-domain piezoelectric crack.** `ШВЕ` is expanded as the energy-release rate. The integral virtual crack-closure method is named explicitly; the reported distinction between crack opening and energy-release-rate sensitivity to interfacial strain continuity is preserved.
2. **Optical crack experiments.** Grammar and terminology were normalized (`поляризаційно-проєкційна`, `ступінчасте навантаження`). The five source photographic figures were replaced by compact TikZ schematics showing the same experimental stages and representative crack paths; no quantitative data were inferred from the photographs.
3. **Craze zones.** The source’s garbled symbols were reconstructed as `μ`, `ν`, `σ_Y`, `σ_{33}^{(m)}`, `σ_{13}^{(m)}`, and `D_3^{(m)}`. The printed Mises condition was regularized to `(σ−σ_1)^2+4τ^2−(4/3)σ_T^2=0`. The two craze intervals `[a_1,a]` and `[b,b_1]` are retained.
4. **Oblique shock wave.** The source momentum equation is incomplete after raster extraction and omits the pressure contribution. It was restored to the conservative Euler form `∂(ρv)/∂t+∇·(ρv⊗v+pI)=0`, consistent with the stated inviscid ideal-gas model and the accompanying mass and energy equations. Source plots were replaced by explicitly schematic TikZ/PGFPlots reconstructions; the reported pressure jump, geometry, incidence angle, and simulation time were preserved.
5. **Turbulent-flow modeling.** The English was substantially edited for grammar and coherence. RANS, LES, DES, and DDES terminology and the author’s conclusion that RANS and hybrid RANS–LES approaches will remain the principal applied tools were retained.
6. **Viscoelastic anisotropic plates.** The small parameter is denoted by `λ` and interpreted, as stated in the source, through the time variation of Poisson’s ratio. `епоксидну` was corrected to `епоксидного матеріалу`; Laurent and Faber expansions, generalized least squares, Volterra substitution, and Rabotnov operators were retained.
7. **Gyrostat with a moving mass.** The affiliation typo `architectur` was corrected to `Architecture`. Fragmented English sentences were rebuilt while preserving the simultaneous effects of a viscous fluid, a damped moving mass, and constant body-fixed torques, together with the averaging-method objective.
